Citron Meringue Recipe


  • Description:

    • A slice of French lemon pie is not something I will easily turn down. No matter how sumptuous a meal may be, there will always be room the refreshing zing of this beautiful dessert.

  • Ingredients:
    • 150g all-purpose flour
    • 75g cold butter, cubed
    • Pinch of fine sea salt
    • 2 tbsps raw cane sugar
    • 4 eggs
    • 90g butter
    • juice of two lemons
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 150g caster sugar
    • 2 egg whites
    • 75g icing sugar

  • Steps:
    1. Heat A Wok With Some Oil In It
    2. Add The Chicken And Season With Salt And Black Pepper. Allow To Cook For A Few Minutes
    3. Add Dark Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Sesame Oil, Fish Sauce And Rice Wine Vinegar To The Chicken
    4. Stir Fry Until Cooked And Keep Aside
    5. Heat The Same Wok With Some Oil In It
    6. Add Carrot And Shredded Cabbage With Some Garlic Chives
    7. Stir Fry For 1 Minute
    8. Add 1 Portion Of Noodle 150g (for 1 Serving)
    9. Flavour The Noodles With Dark Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Fish Sauce, Sesame Oil, Rice Wine Vinegar And Black Pepper
    10. Stir Fry For A Few Mins
    11. Add 1 Handful Of Bok Choy
    12. Add The Cooked Chicken
    13. Add Spring Onion
    14. Add Some Garlic Sauce
    15. Stir Fry The Whole Mixture Until Cooked
    16. At The End, Sprinkle With Chives And Egg Strips
